Dear Deafening Silence

“There are times when we must speak, not because you are going to change the other person, but because if you don’t speak, they have changed you.”

Amidst our nation’s turbulent times, we are led by those who, through powerful activism, inspire hope for a just and racially equal society. Unfortunately, their words and actions at times are met with a deafening silence. Many remain silent for a multitude of reasons, such as not knowing what to say, citizenship status/undocumentation, or fear of retaliation and digital scrutiny.
We believe in encouraging, educating, and empathizing with those afraid to speak up. Dear Deafening Silence, serves as both a podium and a safe haven. Not only do we broadcast your powerful video letters inspiring courage and vulnerability, but we also provide a solution for those who wish to remain anonymous while their sentiments are still heard.
